Product FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What are the steps of transferring a domain name to a different registrar?
1. The account holder for the domain requests (auth-code) for the domain.
2. The registrant provides the accredited registrar with the auth-code.
3. Transfer request is submitted electronically to SaudiNIC.
How long it takes to register or transfer the domain ?
If the procedures were followed correctly and all documents were submitted, the expected time to implement the request is 24 hours.
How can I register a domain name under extensions available to the registrar not requiring additional review by SaudiNIC (com.sa, pub.sa, net.sa, and السعودية.sa)?
1. Filling up the domain registration form.
2. Uploading the documents.
3. Review the request.
4. Administrative contact approval.
5. After attaching the required documents in the correct way, the domain reservation is confirmed within 24 hours only.
How can I register a domain name under extensions available to the registrar but requiring additional review by SaudiNIC (edu.sa, org.sa, med.sa, sch.sa)?
1. Filling Up The Domain Registration Form.
2. Uploading the documents.
3. Review the request.
4. Administrative contact approval.
5. The request is sent and waiting for the Saudi NIC review and approval Within 2 working days
What are the necessary requirements to renew the domain name?
1. Commercial register.
2. The data of the administrative contact shall be correct.
3. The data of the registrant shall be local.
How to do amendments to domain name registration information (Administrative Contact, Technical Contact and nameservers)?
1. Filling up the modification form.
2. Uploading the documents (Special cases).
3. Preparing the domain name hosting (special cases).
4. Review and confirm the request.
5. Administrative contact approval.
How can I transfer a domain name to a different registrant?
1. Filling up the domain transfer form.
2. Current Administrative Contact Approval.
3. Uploading the documents.
4. Review and confirm the request.
5. New Administrative Contact Approval.
What are the steps of Deleting a domain name?
1. Filling up the domain deletion form.
2. Uploading the documents.
3. Review and confirm the request.
4. Administrative Contact Approval.
How can I manage Domain Name System Security Extensions (DNSSEC)?
1. Filling up Enable DNSSEC form.
2. Review the request.
3. Administrative Contact approval.
What are the documents required to register a new domain?
To register a new domain, a copy of the official documents must be attached to prove the relationship between the required domain and the registered exponent in the official documents. The relationship between them has been clarified in order to reserve the domain.
