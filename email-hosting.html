Frequently asked questions

What is email hosting?
Just like with web hosting, email hosting is a service provided by a hosting or email company to manage custom domain-based email accounts. Most companies basically rent out space on an email server to customers, then handle email sending and receiving.
How do I get a company email address?
It’s easy! Let’s see how to get your own email for your domain name with us, and how to make a business email address. First, you need to register a domain name. You can do this with any domain name registrar. But, if your domain is with ProHost and you purchase an email plan, all your records will be automatically set up in most cases. If you registered your domain name somewhere else, it’s called a 3rd party domain name. But don’t worry. You can still enjoy an email plan with us and easily set this up in a few clicks. You’ll get the steps sent to you personally.
Does ProHost offer free email forwarding?
Email forwarding is a feature included in your M365 Email subscription from Prohost. Use email forwarding to create email aliases for specific business purposes (e.g., info@yourcompany.com, sales@ support@). Simply forward those to your main email account and all messages get delivered to a single email box.
What are the benefits of email hosting?
With our hosted email service, you are renting high-quality email servers to manage the communications of your business.
