Product FAQs

Frequently asked questions

What are the benefits of Dedicated Hosting?
With Dedicated, you get complete isolation on a physical server for your hosting. Your Virtual Private Server will be unaffected by other users and with root access you can install whatever applications you require on your server. The CPU, RAM, HDD, and Bandwidth allocated to your Dedicated Hosting package will always be available for your applications. A Dedicated server gives you all the features of a Dedicated server at an affordable price.
What is KVM Dedicated Server Technology?
KVM (Kernel-based Virtual Machine) is a type of Virtualization interface in Linux servers which ensures fantastic performance and security while guaranteeing highly optimized resource isolation. Use of KVM in our Dedicated Servers allows us to deliver tremendous processing power and throughput that your applications / websites need.
Can I get additional Dedicated IPs on the Dedicated Server?
Yes, you can purchase dedicated IPs at an additional cost.
How does web hosting work?
Prohost provides complete support on all our Dedicated Hosting Plans for issues that are related to Boot, Network, Hardware, Login and Re-installation of the VM. This also includes basic firewall setup and preliminary investigation of any system exploits. For the complete scope of support, please refer to the technical specifications section.
Is an Upgrade or Downgrade possible between the Dedicated Hosting Plans?
Yes, you can now easily upgrade your plan from the control panel by simply selecting the new plan and paying. The amount you have already paid for the current plan will get adjusted on a pro rate basis. Currently, this upgrade option is available only on our India based servers. The downgrade option is not available for any of the KVM Dedicated plans/servers at this time.
Do you have managed services for Dedicated hosting??
Yes. We have Managed Dedicated Hosting services available for our new & existing customers. Managed hosting services can be purchased as an add-on feature with your server.
